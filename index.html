About Us

Our Story

AGNES' Library was established in 2010 with the mission to provide accessible knowledge resources to the community of Kakoba, Mbarara and surrounding areas. Named after Agnes Nakiwala, a passionate educator who dedicated her life to literacy, our library continues her legacy.

We believe that access to information and knowledge is a fundamental right that empowers individuals and transforms communities. Our library serves as a hub for learning, creativity, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To be the leading community library in Western Uganda, fostering a culture of reading, lifelong learning, and intellectual curiosity.
